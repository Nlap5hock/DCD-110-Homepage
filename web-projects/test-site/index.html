Reflection 2

In chapter 3 of Maisa Imamović's "The Psychology of The Web Developer", she discusses the idea of Presentation websites, which she describes as digital containers that display a subject in a marketable and structured way. The rely heavily on templates, grids, categorization, and a clear hiarchy of information. Maisa also discusses real-time websites. These websites create immersion through interaction by allowing users to "write" as well as read the site in a controlled system. In chapter 4 she goes in-depth into the topic of the "simple" website.
